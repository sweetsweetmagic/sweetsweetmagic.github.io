Influence design language

It is a concept design of UI&UX(micro interaction) named 「Influence Design」. It's overview is also included in my works collection, and this is the detailed design rules. I designed it with the concept of cell and cell differentiation, and tried to build the most direct visual image through the concept of "cell", so that users can predict in advance what might happen next judge whether it is the same as the expected result, and judge the current situation from the status of interaction elements.
